# Privacy Policy

**ScanMyReceipt**
Last updated: 5 March 2026

## Summary

ScanMyReceipt does not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties. All your data stays on your device.

## Data Storage

All receipt data — including scanned images, shop names, dates, amounts, and collection settings — is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server, cloud service, or external database.

## Camera Access

ScanMyReceipt uses your device's camera solely to scan paper receipts. Images captured by the camera are processed on-device using Apple's built-in text recognition (OCR) and saved locally on your device. Camera images are never transmitted, shared, or uploaded.

## Data Sharing

When you use the export feature (PDF, CSV, or UBL), the app generates files on your device and presents the standard iOS share sheet. You choose where and how to share those files. ScanMyReceipt does not initiate any data transfer on its own.

## Third-Party Services

ScanMyReceipt does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting services. The app is built entirely with Apple's frameworks and has zero external dependencies.

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app. ScanMyReceipt has no access to your data after uninstallation.

## Children's Privacy

ScanMyReceipt does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
